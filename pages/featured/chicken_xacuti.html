Authentic Chicken Xacuti Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces (with bone)
  • 1 cup freshly grated coconut
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 4-5 dried red chilies
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick
  • 3-4 cloves
  • 1 star anise
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg powder
  • 2 tbsp poppy seeds
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Dry roast red chilies, coriander seeds, cumin seeds, peppercorns, cinnamon, cloves, and star anise until aromatic. Cool.
  2. Grind the roasted spices with grated coconut, poppy seeds, nutmeg powder, and some water to a fine paste.
  3. Heat coconut oil in a heavy-bottom pan. SautÃ© onions until translucent and golden.
  4. Add the spice-coconut paste and fry for 5-7 minutes until the oil starts to separate.
  5. Add chicken pieces, tamarind paste, salt, and mix well.
  6. Add water to cover the chicken partially. Cook on medium heat until chicken is tender and the gravy thickens.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or bread.
