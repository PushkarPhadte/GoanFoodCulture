Authentic Goan Fish Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g firm white fish (pomfret, kingfish)
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 tbsp tamarind water or kokum
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander

Steps

  1. Grind coconut, chilies, turmeric, and chili powder with a little water to make a smooth masala paste.
  2. Heat coconut oil in a pan, sautÃ© onions until golden, then add tomatoes.
  3. Add masala paste; fry for 2-3 minutes until aromatic.
  4. Pour in tamarind water, simmer, then add fish pieces and salt.
  5. Simmer gently until fish is cooked and flavors meld. Garnish with coriander.
  6. Serve hot with Goan red rice or pao bread.
